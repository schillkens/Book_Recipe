Ingredients
Sweet Potatoes and 5 eggs, cooking oil.
1.	Peel off the sweet potatoes and have them on cooking gas for 30 mins.
2.	With cooked potatoes, smash using the mortar.
3.	Prepare the eggs on the dish and add quarter spoon of the salt and stir .
4.	Have smashed potatoes in a dish and mix with eggs in the dish and stir for the mixture to be thick.
